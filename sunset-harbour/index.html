The Community

Coastal living on the Intracoastal Waterway

Sunset Harbour sits directly on the Intracoastal Waterway in the Cherry Grove section of North Myrtle Beach, just off Highway 17 and Sea Mountain Highway. Past the fountains at the entrance, palm-lined streets wind through custom-built homes — from raised beach cottages to grand waterway-front residences with private docks.

The waterfront amenity center anchors the community: a saltwater pool overlooking the waterway, a clubhouse with day dock, private boat ramp, two fishing piers, a screened gazebo, and a separate storage lot for boats and trailers.

And the location is the closer: shopping and dining within walking distance, and Cherry Grove Beach a golf cart ride from your driveway. If you want true waterway living with the ocean minutes away, Sunset Harbour is the address.

Good to Know

Sunset Harbour questions, answered

Where is Sunset Harbour located?

Sunset Harbour is in the Cherry Grove section of North Myrtle Beach, South Carolina, directly on the Intracoastal Waterway just off Highway 17 and Sea Mountain Highway.

Does Sunset Harbour have boat access?

Yes. The community has a private boat ramp on the Intracoastal Waterway, a day dock at the waterfront clubhouse, two fishing piers, and a separate storage lot for boats, trailers, and RVs.

What amenities does Sunset Harbour offer?

A saltwater swimming pool overlooking the Intracoastal Waterway, a waterfront clubhouse with day dock, two fishing piers, a screened gazebo, and dedicated boat and RV storage.

How close is Sunset Harbour to the beach?

Cherry Grove Beach is just minutes away — many residents make the trip by golf cart. Shopping and dining on Highway 17 are within walking distance.

What schools serve Sunset Harbour?

Sunset Harbour is zoned for Ocean Drive Elementary School, North Myrtle Beach Middle School, and North Myrtle Beach High School.
