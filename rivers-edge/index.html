The Community

A quiet address on the Waccamaw River

Rivers Edge Plantation is a quiet, gated community along the Waccamaw River, just off Highway 90 in Conway. Residents enjoy a riverside amenity center with a large swimming pool, sun deck, community clubhouse, and a private boat landing with a day dock right on the river — perfect for boating, fishing, or simply soaking in the water views.

Tree-lined streets and a peaceful, semi-rural setting give the neighborhood a true small-town feel, while a short twenty-minute drive puts you in downtown Conway, at Coastal Carolina University, or on your way to Myrtle Beach and North Myrtle Beach.

If you want river access and a quiet, established neighborhood away from the tourist bustle, Rivers Edge Plantation fits the bill.

Good to Know

Rivers Edge Plantation questions, answered

Is Rivers Edge Plantation a gated community?

Yes. Both entrances to Rivers Edge Plantation are gated. The community sits in a quiet, semi-rural setting just off Highway 90 in Conway, South Carolina.

Does Rivers Edge Plantation have river access?

Yes. Residents have a private community boat landing and a day dock directly on the Waccamaw River, ideal for boating, fishing, and kayaking.

What amenities does Rivers Edge Plantation offer?

A riverside amenity center with a large swimming pool and sun deck, a community clubhouse, a playground, and the private boat landing with day dock on the Waccamaw River.

What schools serve Rivers Edge Plantation?

Rivers Edge Plantation is zoned for Waccamaw Elementary School, Black Water Middle School, and Carolina Forest High School.

How far is Rivers Edge Plantation from Myrtle Beach?

About 15 to 20 minutes via International Drive. Downtown Conway, Coastal Carolina University, and Conway Medical Center are each roughly 20 minutes away.
