The Community

Resort living in the heart of Carolina Forest

Behind Waterbridge's brick-and-wrought-iron gates, lantern-lit streets wind through coastal cottage-style custom homes set on wooded homesites and panoramic lakefronts. The community wraps more than 80 acres of lakes and is bordered by roughly 120 acres of federally protected woodland — a third of the neighborhood will never be developed.

The amenity village is the showpiece: the largest residential swimming pool in South Carolina — Junior Olympic lap lanes, swim-up refreshment center, jetted spa, and a landscaped island — beside a clubhouse with catering kitchen, a 24-hour fitness center, and courts for tennis, pickleball, basketball, and sand volleyball.

And then there's Palmetto Lake: sixty acres of open water in the middle of it all, with a community boat launch and storage — kayaks, paddleboards, and slow evening cruises just beyond your backyard.
